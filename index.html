About Us

It all started on the ground. We cherish our ballooning roots as a family-run business. And so we became the only hot air balloon ride company in Central Texas to offer a ground tour, giving guests the full story behind how we started and what we do here at OnAir Ballooning. Now that's free flight! OnAir Ballooning offers the most comprehensive hot air ballooning experiences in Arizona. From scenic sunset and full moon rises to landing into a remote cow pasture and setting up camp, we offer several unique packages to ensure your dreams of floating over the Grand Canyon or Las Vegas come true.
